White Bread for the bread machine

Simple bread machine white bread and tastes great!

The good and classic recipe of white bread,that get the job done,and that will goes good with any soup!

ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Place water,sugar,salt,oil,bread flour,and yeast into the pan of the bread machine
  2. Bake on white Bread setting
  3. Cool on wire racks before slicing
